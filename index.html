Gerardo Rozin

Argentinian Producer and Journalist who made everyone a little happier

Gerardo Rozín with Jesica Cirio in a TV show called "La peña de Morfi"

Gerardo Rozín (Rosario, June 18, 1970-Buenos Aires, March 11, 2022) was an Argentine journalist, producer and radio and television presenter. 1 He was mainly recognized for having been the host of several television programs such as Gracias por venir, gracias por estar, Morfi, todos a la mesa y La peña de Morfi, on the Telefe channel.
